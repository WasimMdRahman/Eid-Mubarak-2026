# **App Name**: Eid Veil

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Curtain Presentation: Implement a visually striking landing page with two elegant curtains, featuring deep green or maroon hues and subtle gold fringe, meeting to reveal a centered date.
- Scroll-Triggered Curtain Reveal: Enable smooth, synchronized curtain animation where the curtains slide off-screen upon user scroll, leveraging CSS transitions or GSAP for a high-end feel.
- Interactive Eid Greeting Display: Behind the animated curtains, elegantly display the 'Eid Mubarak' greeting with large, glowing typography against a celebratory background featuring subtle patterns.
- Responsive Design for All Devices: Ensure the entire website design and animations adapt seamlessly and remain visually appealing across various screen sizes, from mobile to desktop.
- Gold Fringe Pattern Tool: Provide a generative AI tool to suggest and visualize unique 'gold fringe' border patterns for the inner edges of the curtains, enhancing the aesthetic wow factor.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a rich, deep green (#105926), selected for its profound and celebratory elegance, symbolizing prosperity often associated with Eid festivities. It forms the base of the luxurious curtain design.
- The background color is a heavily desaturated, very dark green (#16241b), providing a subtle depth that beautifully complements the primary curtain color and reinforces the sophisticated, moody aesthetic.
- The accent color is a vibrant, clear gold (#FAE642), chosen to provide the requested 'gold accents.' It delivers strong contrast and a sense of luxury, ideal for highlights, borders, and the 'glowing' typography.
- For the prominent date display, 'Literata' (serif) will provide a sophisticated and slightly formal aesthetic, enhancing its importance as a centerpiece. For the main 'Eid Mubarak' greeting, 'Playfair Display' (modern sans-serif) is chosen for its elegant, high-contrast, and fashionable feel, perfect for large, glowing typography.
- The revealed background will subtly incorporate thematic elements such as delicate crescent moons, stars, or elegant geometric patterns, chosen to enhance the festive and celebratory ambiance without distracting from the main greeting.
- The landing view features two prominent, 'position: fixed' or 'sticky' curtains covering the screen, meeting in the middle and forming an inverted 'A' shape at the bottom. The central gap strategically showcases the date. The reveal behind utilizes a full-width, centered greeting for maximum impact.
- Curtain movement is designed for smoothness and high-end feel. Utilizing CSS transitions or GSAP, the scroll-triggered animation will make curtains fluidly slide left and right, ensuring a synchronized and immersive reveal sequence.
